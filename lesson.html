Lesson Overview

Our lessons are designed to be flexible and practical, making learning enjoyable for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. We offer customized lessons tailored to individual needs, including daily conversation, pronunciation correction, and lessons for children.

Goal

Our aim is to help you speak Japanese naturally and acquire practical skills that you can use in real-life situations and at work.

Who is this for?

✔ Those who are learning Japanese for the first time
✔ Those who want to improve their conversation skills
✔ Parents who want to support their children's Japanese learning

Pricing

50 minutes – $23 USD (Available as many times per week as you like)

About the Tutor

I am a Certified Japanese Teacher with over 1,600 hours of online teaching experience. Additionally, I spent one year as a volunteer teacher in the Philippines.

With a friendly and relaxed atmosphere, I provide personalized learning plans tailored to each student’s needs, ensuring that learning Japanese is both enjoyable and effective.

I especially enjoy teaching shy learners and children!
